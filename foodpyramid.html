What's a food Pyramid? Why's it important?

Definition:

A food pyramid is a triangular diagram representing the optimal number of servings to
be eaten each day from each of the basic food groups.

It's very important that you should focus on learning the meaning of the food pyramid
because it helps people to understand how many servings on what certain foods you should have to stay healthy.
