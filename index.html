Why is it important that we need to know about the food we eat?
Our food choices that we eat will depend on how healthy they can be.

Let's take the sections of our foods as an example of our servings we should take.

Fruits

Depending on the size of the fruit, these are the servings as follows:

  • medium sized fruits(apple,orange,pair, or banana):1 serving.

  • small fruits(plums, kiwis or mandarin oranges):2 servings.

Vegetables

  • cooked vegetables (fresh or frozen): 1/2 cup.

  • bowl of salad (lettuce, tomato, cucumber): 1 serving.

  • bowl of homemade vegetable soup: 1 serving.

Dairies

  • Milk: 1 glass (200ml)

  • Yogurt: 1 carton (125g)

  • yogurt drink: 1 bottle (200ml)

  • hard or semi-hard cheese such as cheddar or edam: 2 thumbs (25g)

  • soft cheese such as brie or camembert: 2 thumbs (25g)
